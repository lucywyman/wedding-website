Location, Location, Location

The Laurelhurst Club

Located at 3721 SE Ankeny St, directly across from Laurelhurst Park.

Getting There

By Bus

The venue is walking distance from several bus lines. If possible we highly encourage folks to take public transit! Bonus: you won't need a designated driver

  • 20 - Burnside/Stark line
  • 66 - Marquam Hill/Hollywood
  • 75 - Cesar Chavez/Lombard
Parking

The venue doesn't have a parking lot, but there's a good amount of parking near the venue. Cars can park in front of the club as well as across the street in front of Laurelhurst Park. You can also park anywhere in the neighborhood if there's no parking immediately by the club. We highly encourage folks to carpool!

Booze

We will have beer, wine, and champagne available at the wedding. The venue doesn't allow distilled spirits, but if you'd like to bring your own fermented beverage our bartender can serve it.

Tiki Torches

We regret to say that the venue does not allow tiki torches. We're sorry for any inconvenience this may cause.
